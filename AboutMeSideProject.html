Life at RifleGear:
The Range Side

For the past 4 and a half years, I have worked at a gun store in North Texas called RifleGear. Over these past 4 years, I have learned and grown a lot as a young adult. This job is what I believe to be my first stepping stone into a more serious & professional role in my career path. I have learned how to remain calm in high stress enviorments and how to quickly think on my feet; when faced with potentially dangerous, life changing circumstances out on the range. There have even been times where I've even had live firearms pointed at me!! (Not malciously, but due to a "new shooter"s ignorance on gun safety.)

Going through these expereciences has certainly changed me for the better, and has taught me to be patient with people who are new to the sport / hobby. Afterall, everyone has to start somewhere. Through understanding that, I believe that I have been able to apply that same patience into my personal life. Such as with my friends and family. Whether it be helping my grandma figure out how to send an email on her iPhone, getting rid of small bugs and rodents (that absolutely terrify my girlfriend!) in our apartment, to helping my friends get into the hobby and sports of firearms.
